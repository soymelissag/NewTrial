About la abuela

Abuela 8 Ball is the world's most opinionated fortune teller. Ask any question, pick what is weighing on your heart, and shake the bola. She answers in her own words — half blessing, half regañada (scolding), always with love.

  • 1. Write your question. Be honest, she always knows when you're not.
  • 2. Pick a topic: amor, dinero, familia, or la vida.
  • 3. Shake the bola and receive your consejo.

Not a substitute for actually calling your real grandmother. Llámala. She misses you.
